Make the most of the tour by taking advantage of hotel pickup and return. During the excursion, you will visit a natural swimming pool, indulge in snacks, and enjoy the guidance of a knowledgeable guide. Experience the thrill of riding a speedboat and a catamaran while exploring the beautiful Saona Island.

Once on the island, you'll have a range of activities to choose from. Take a refreshing swim in the turquoise waters, visit the open bar for some drinks, relax on beach hammocks, stroll along the shoreline, or engage in a game of volleyball.

A delicious Dominican lunch awaits, featuring a variety of dishes such as chicken, pork, rice, beans, salad, and fruit. If you opt for the VIP option, you can even savor lobster and premium beverages. Take your time to fully immerse yourself in the island's beauty, participating in all the activities offered.

Overall, this tour provides a comprehensive experience with hotel transportation, natural swimming, exhilarating boat rides, a buffet-style lunch, and various leisure options on Saona Island.
